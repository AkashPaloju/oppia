Have your own account

You don't need to register to start using Oppia, but having your account allows you to track your progress, save lessons, set goals, and master skills in your favorite subjects!

For Teachers

Oppia provides students with access to a variety of lessons and different styles of learning to keep them engaged and interested. Through our Learner Groups feature you can assign lessons and follow your student's progress.

For Parents

Oppia is a free tool that makes learning fun through age-appropriate, ad-free lessons! You can also see your child's progress through our Learner Group feature.

For Volunteers

Oppia is a non-profit project built by volunteers around the world. We always need more people to create, improve and translate lessons, contributing graphics and more!
